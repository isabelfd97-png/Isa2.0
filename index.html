About Isa

Isa is an intelligent career analysis tool powered by Claude AI and Telegram. Send job descriptions and get strategic insights about the job market, repeated skills, AI-related signals, and culture indicators.

Key Features

  • Analyze multiple job descriptions in one message
  • Identify repeated skills across positions
  • Detect AI-related signals in job postings
  • Analyze company culture signals (pressure vs stability)
  • Get personalized strategic insights
